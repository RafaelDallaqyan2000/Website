Personal life

Elon Musk was born in 1971 in Pretoria, South Africa. When Elon was ten years old, his parents - a Canadian model and a South African electrical engineer - divorced, the children (and Musk had a brother and sister) began to live mainly with their father in Africa. According to the Washington Post, Musk's father was a tough man and treated the children accordingly, for example, forcing them to sit silently for hours and listen to his moralizing. The situation was no better at school. Classmates laughed and mocked the withdrawn nerd Elon, and once beat him so hard that he had to repair a broken nose in the hospital. Musk became more and more withdrawn into himself, his outlet was the computer.

Mask family

Elon Musk has been married three times (two of them to the same woman). He met his first wife, writer Justine Wilson, while studying in Canada. The couple has five large sons. With his second wife, actress Talulah Riley, the entrepreneur met in London a little later than the start of the divorce proceedings with his ex-wife. They did not have children during their relationship. After divorcing in January 2012, the couple got back together and remarried a year later, only to end their marriage permanently in 2014. Now Elon is in a relationship with 33-year-old Canadian singer Grimes, who recently gave birth to his son.
